Computer programmers are most often employed by the Computer Systems Design industry. The average yearly wage for Computer programmers was $85,018 in 2016. In 2016, Computer programmers had a wage GINI coefficient of 0.309, which is lower than than the national average of 0.479. In other words, wages are distributed more evenly for Computer programmers (shown in orange) than for the overall labor force (shown in blue).
